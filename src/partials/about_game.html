About the Game

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Super Flash Speed Hero, where you take on the role of a legendary speedster, battling ruthless criminals and extremist threats in an action-packed ...
 open-world adventure. If you've ever dreamed of harnessing the incredible power of super speed to fight crime and bring justice to the city, this game is your ultimate playground.

In Super Flash Speed Hero, you will embark on high-speed rescue missions, racing through the city to protect innocent civilians from the clutches of dangerous...
mobsters, gangsters, and an underground criminal syndicate. With lightning-fast reflexes and extraordinary abilities, you must outmaneuver your enemies, deliver powerful strikes, and restore peace to a city under siege.
